About Me

Thoughtful Communicator.
Curious Mind.

Hi, I am an English Honours graduate trained in Literature — shaped by questions and deeply interested in how words, people, and ideas intersect. Drawn to nature, open roads, and new experiences, I vehemently believe that meaning isn't found in one lane.

My academic training has sharpened my ability to analyze ideas, articulate perspectives clearly, and engage with complexity in a structured manner. I have a strong foundation in communication, content development, and leadership.

Through public speaking, debate, and elocution — along with leadership training programs focused on structured social work — I've developed clarity of thought, teamwork, and people-management skills. I've also worked on community outreach initiatives, including teaching underprivileged children, which strengthened my sense of responsibility and social awareness.
